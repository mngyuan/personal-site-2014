I'm a maker.

Making, building, breaking - these are my bread and butter. Whether it's music I'm recording, software I'm building, or physical stuff I'm making, for me there is no greater pleasure than creating and learning new things. I'm 19, a CompSci major at UC Berkeley, and I know there's always space in life to keep learning. Welcome to my website.

Projects/WIP

A collection of my projects, personal and school- or work-related. Professionally, I'm passionate about game programming and design, software QA, and development, of websites, apps, or other products that affect everyday life.




h

kml/howmanygiven

Made with a buddy partially for BearHack 2013, this website is an expansion of an idea from a project we had in a class. It analyzes the plaintext content of a website and spits out a "sentiment level", among other things.

r

berkeley/rnd

A website collaboratively built for a club that friends and I started. We post personal project ideas and invite other students to organize groups of students to make those projects.

v

kml/vypyr

A collection of guitar pedal and audio mods I'm in the process of making. Currently I'm making two pedalShields and coding a compression effect for them, a wavetable Arduino Due synth, and a simple custom stompbox for my Peavy Vypyr 15W guitar amp. This should all be done, with sound demos, early to mid February.



You can find more of my in-progess personal projects on my github. There's a text-adventure game in Python, a helper utility for Binding of Isaac, a web scraper for Berkeley courses, and half of a game I made with friends. I've made a few things and I'm looking to make more.
