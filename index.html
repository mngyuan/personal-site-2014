I'm a maker.

Making, building, breaking - these are my bread and butter. Whether it's music I'm recording, software I'm building, or (physical stuff) I'm making, for me there is no greater pleasure than creating and learning new things. I'm 19, a CompSci major at Berkeley, and I know there's always space in life to keep learning - I'm nowhere near knowing as much as I'd like. Welcome to my website.
